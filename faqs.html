Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sell a product on Swapify?

You can list your product by visiting the "Sell Your Product" page and submitting your product details and images. Once approved by the admin, your product will go live.

How long does it take for my product to be approved?

Product approvals can take up to 24 hours. You'll receive a notification once your product is live.

Is it free to use Swapify?

Yes, creating an account and listing products is free. There are no upfront fees.

How do I track my orders?

Once logged in, go to "My Orders" to track the status and view your order history.
